About ClearTable

Privacy

Audio enhancement runs on this phone only — nothing is recorded or uploaded.

The transcript uses your browser's speech engine; depending on the browser, audio may be sent to Apple/Google/etc. for transcription. Turn Auto off in the transcript panel to keep speech on-device.

Keep your phone awake

If iPhone sleeps, set Settings > Display & Brightness > Auto-Lock to Never while testing, then change it back.

Microphone source

Best results: iPhone built-in mic + headset for output. iOS Safari often forces the headset's mic when one is connected — a native iOS app would be more reliable.

Tips for restaurants
  • Phone face-up near the centre of the table.
  • Away from cutlery and glassware.
  • Keep Safari in the foreground.
Background audio

This web prototype pauses if the screen sleeps or you switch apps. A native iOS app could keep listening with the screen off.

Not a medical device

ClearTable is an experimental prototype, not a hearing aid. Use comfortable volumes only.

Limits
  • ~150–400 ms delay through Bluetooth.
  • Single-mic EQ + dynamics. No AI denoiser, no beamforming.
